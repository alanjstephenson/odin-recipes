Chocolate balls recipe

Check out this super simple recipe for a tasty healthy treat!

Do you love chocolate? Do you also feel guilty about eating it? Well no more...

These are a super simple, super quick to make healthy alternative and they better than what you're trying to replace. So, what are you waiting for?

Recipe

Equipment

  • 1 mixing bowl
  • 1 food processor
  • 1 wooden spoon
  • 1 tablespoon
  • 1 tray

Ingredients

  • 200g of almonds
  • 3 tbsp of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup fo cacao powder

Instructions

  1. Empty 200g of almonds into the food processor and blend until you have finely ground almond
  2. Pour the almond meal into the mixing bowl along 3 the maple syrup and cacoa powder and mix until combined and covered
  3. Roll the mixture into 12 balls placing each on a tray and refrigerate

Tips!

  1. Add 1/4 tsp of sea salt and 1/4 tsp of pure vanilla for enhances flavour
  2. You can use this mixture to make brownies, just form the mixture into squares
  3. Roll them in superfoods including coconut, goji berries, hemp seeds or cacao nibs
